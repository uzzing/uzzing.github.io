I studied examtopic dump(574 questions) twice and tried to analyze and remember some keywords on each question. Some questions have the specific pattern like having same keywords. And When I take a test, I exactly felt it and I did good study.

For studying AWS SAA, I could know and understand flow of AWS and how to use many AWS solutions easily and efficiently. I totally can sure that AWS SAA will help our backend development skill improve.
